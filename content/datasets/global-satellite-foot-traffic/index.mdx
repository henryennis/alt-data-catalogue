Orbital Insight's foot traffic dataset combines satellite imagery analysis with anonymized mobile device signals to produce daily visitor estimates across 500,000+ retail locations in the US, Europe, and Asia-Pacific. The data is aggregated at the store level with no personally identifiable information.

## Signal Construction

Each observation includes a normalized visit count, year-over-year delta, and a confidence score indicating signal strength. The normalization adjusts for device panel composition changes, seasonal weather patterns, and public holidays to isolate true consumer behavior shifts.

<Methodology>
1. Raw mobile pings are collected from 80+ data partners via SDK integrations
2. Pings are geofenced against 523K+ retail polygons with 15-meter accuracy
3. Raw counts are normalized against a rolling 365-day baseline to remove panel drift
4. Year-over-year deltas are computed using the same-day-of-week methodology to account for weekday/weekend patterns
5. A confidence score (0-1) is assigned based on device density within the geofence, satellite revisit frequency, and historical signal stability
</Methodology>

## Coverage Details

The dataset covers three primary geographies with varying depth:

- **United States**: 412,000+ locations across all 50 states. Coverage density is highest in metropolitan statistical areas (MSAs) with population > 250K. Rural coverage (population < 50K) carries lower confidence scores.
- **Europe**: 78,000+ locations across UK, Germany, France, Netherlands, and Nordics. Coverage expanded to Southern Europe in Q3 2023.
- **Asia-Pacific**: 33,000+ locations across Japan, South Korea, Australia, and Singapore. Tier 1 cities only before 2021; Tier 2 expansion ongoing.

<Callout type="info" title="Panel Composition">
The underlying device panel represents approximately 8-12% of the adult population in US markets and 4-7% in international markets. Normalization algorithms adjust for panel size fluctuations to maintain signal consistency over time.
</Callout>

## Data Quality Notes

<Callout type="warning" title="Known Coverage Gaps">
Rural areas with population density below 10,000 per square mile may exhibit confidence scores below 0.5. We recommend filtering to `confidence >= 0.7` for quantitative strategies and `confidence >= 0.5` for broader market research.
</Callout>

The <FieldRef>ticker</FieldRef> field is populated for approximately 91.6% of locations. Unmapped locations are typically independent retailers, franchisees without a public parent, or newly opened stores awaiting entity resolution. The mapping is refreshed weekly.

<Callout type="tip" title="Backtest Best Practice">
When backtesting with this dataset, always use the <FieldRef>date</FieldRef> field as your as-of date rather than the delivery timestamp. The data is point-in-time correct -- each record reflects what was known on that date, with no future information leaking into historical observations. The 7-day revision window means only the most recent week of data may change; anything older is immutable.
</Callout>
